About Me

Since you're dying to know who the mastermind behind this website is..
I first started this food blog about 4 days ago, because, well... I had to, and I needed to come up with a web app idea to show off for my Q2 project! The purpose of our Q2 project was to showcase my design skills, as well as show off how amazing I am at the backend of webdev. Luckily for me, I had previously decided to start a instagram food blog, as seen on my blog page. After a few days of working on this app, my interest in the site tapered off as I realized this is much harder than it looks. Nonetheless, I had to power through and finish this darn app as best as I could. So, here we are. It might not be pretty, and it might not really work, but I have no option but to show it off as is and hopefully make it better as I improve my web dev skills.
